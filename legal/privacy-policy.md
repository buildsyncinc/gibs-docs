# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Eitiri AB ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Gibs API and related services ("Service").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you register, we collect:
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Company name (optional)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe)

### 1.2 Usage Data
When you use the Service, we collect:
- API request metadata (timestamps, endpoints, response times)
- Request counts and patterns
- Error logs

### 1.3 What We Do NOT Store
- **Raw query content**: We do not permanently store the content of your API requests
- **Personally identifiable information in queries**: We recommend not including PII in your requests

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Monitor usage and enforce rate limits
- Improve Service quality and accuracy
- Communicate important updates
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Data Processing

### 3.1 Query Processing
- Queries are processed in real-time to generate responses
- We use hashed identifiers (not raw content) for analytics
- Query content is not used to train models

### 3.2 Audit Logs
We maintain audit logs containing:
- Request ID
- Timestamp
- Endpoint called
- Response metadata (confidence, sources cited)
- Processing time

Audit logs are retained for 12 months for compliance purposes.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Clerk**: Authentication
- **Cloudflare**: CDN and security
- **Cohere/Anthropic**: AI processing (query content only during processing)

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal process
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or security issues

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- API key hashing (bcrypt)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security reviews

---

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account information | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Audit logs | 12 months |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Usage analytics | 90 days |

---

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

### 7.1 Access
Request a copy of your personal data.

### 7.2 Rectification
Correct inaccurate personal data.

### 7.3 Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

### 7.4 Data Portability
Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

### 7.5 Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 7.6 Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@gibs.dev.

---

## 8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in:
- Sweden (primary)
- EU/EEA (infrastructure)
- United States (AI providers, with appropriate safeguards)

We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers as required by GDPR.

---

## 9. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only:
- Authentication session cookies
- Security cookies (CSRF protection)

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
